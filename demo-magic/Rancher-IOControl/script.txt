In this demo I'm going to show you a feature of Portworx Enterprise called IO Control.

IO Control allows us to put limits on IOs and Bandwidth of our volumes by configuring paramaters on a storageclass, or by 
setting a limit on existing volumes.

This ensures that volumes cannot cause cluster wide performance problems by over-utilizing IO, which is sometimes called
a "Noisy Neighbor"
